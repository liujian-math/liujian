About me

    I am a postdoc in Mathematics at the Shanghai Jiao Tong University(July 2021--). My postdoc mentor is Prof. Pu Zhang. I finished my PhD in Mathematics at the University of Science and Technology of China (September 2016--June 2021 ). From August 15 2019 to Ocotber 9 2020, I visited the University of Utah. My advisors were Prof. Xiao-Wu Chen and Prof. Srikanth Iyengar. My research interests are derived categories, commutative algebras and representation theory of finite dimensional algebras.
